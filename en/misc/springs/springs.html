Springs 

 The river begins to freeze when the cooler comes
 Her vapours send us a farewell message
 Like saying you'll not see me
 Till the warmer's return
 Till springs
 Till waves
 Till birds
 Till leaves' gay rustle.



The winter whines about the sky
Sending not the snow but the rain
The drops like corpses of snowflakes
Dangling from pines haughty and vain.



As spring comes I move
To a small summer-house
Where my friend spider
Spins his web of time
While my silly machine
Dreams of computing
How long it is.



Trees are the hands of the earth,
Stretched out to the sky in a silent prayer:
Brother sky, protect your sister earth
From these two-legged creatures.
